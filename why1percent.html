We are the future of Cleaning.

Your 1% act, when multiplied by millions of people, can transform the world.

1% believes that using plastic packaging just once is fundamentally wrong. So we’re making it our mission to totally rethink plastic by having sustainability and circular economy as focal point. 

Cleaning industry is usually a low engagement category. Unlike any other existing traditional company, we want to break the stereotypes & create high interest with our branding, create premium value for purchase while throwing light on being environmentally conscious.

Only a combined effort can help humanity achieve the zero-emissions goal over the next years. We believe home is where it counts.

We value the effectiveness of our products and the quality of our formulas to combine performance with respect for our consumers and for the planet.

We have named our brand as 1%, which means we produce and sell power pods [1/100] that is diluted in just tap water [99/100] which then gives100% impact in effective cleaning. Subsequently, your purchase in one reusable bottles replaces several 100 purchases.

We don’t want to ship water, so we reduce lots of co2 emissions throughout supply chain. Also using ocean waste plastic as resource will reduce 56% of Co2 emissions when compared to using virgin plastic. 1% is easy to use and ecofriendly. This is the future of cleaning. Our range of home cleaning products will keep all your surfaces super clean!
